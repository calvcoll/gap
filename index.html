The following is part of my graduate attribute portfolio, this includes my goals, my current skills, and an evaulation of what I need to do to enable myself to reach the goals that I have set out for myself.

This document is written in HTML, without much CSS or JS, to enable myself to easily change the document without to many errors to worry about due to design problems. However this design choice, desires a bit of time to design from the ideas to a page, as the whole structure will have to be generated for this coursework, which will then be easily edittable after the huge commitment of time. This also comes with the benefit that it can run on most web browsers, back to 1995, due to it being complient to the HTML specification, with closed tags, or self-closing tags being used.

Due to the nature of HTML this document will be a static document, however, it is easy to change and modify on the fly with basic understanding of HTML, and since the pages are separated, it shall be easy to find most parts with the hyperlinks placed on the text.
